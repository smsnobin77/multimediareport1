Ubuntu studio is a free and open source software collection. Here have different types of software for audio, graphics, video, photography and publishing tool.

For audio, there have JACK, Ardour, Sequencers and Synthesizers, Virtual Guitar Amps, Jack Session, Audio Programming tools.
For graphics, there have Blender, Inkscape, GIMP, MyPaint, PikoPixel.
For video, there have Openshot, FFMPEG, DVDStyler.
For Photography, there have Darktable and Shotwell.
For Publishing, there have Calibre, Scribus and LibreOffice.
